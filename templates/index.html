ABOUT

Because of recent events such as Covid-19 etc, a large amount of medical data such as CT scan, X-ray, etc. are generating everyday.

All medical sector leaning towards Artificial intelligence, but they do not want to deep dive into technicality of machine Learning.

So, we made this portal where user can generate their own model by providing their own dataset without going into complexities of code for deep learning.
